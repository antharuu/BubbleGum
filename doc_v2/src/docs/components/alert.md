## Alert

Alerts are a good way to highlight a message.

```html
<p _alert="info">Hello in blue!</p>

<p _alert="dark">Hello in dark!</p>

<p _alert="red">Hello in red!</p>
```

<p _alert="info">Hello in blue!</p>

<p _alert="dark">Hello in dark!</p>

<p _alert="red">Hello in red!</p>

You can use, as often, all colors.
